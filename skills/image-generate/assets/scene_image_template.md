# Scene Image Template

## Best For

- session key moments
- confrontation beats
- emotionally loaded dialogue scenes

## Prompt Structure

Dramatic Moment:
- what is happening right now
- who is present
- what is at stake

Blocking:
- who dominates the frame
- who is close, distant, hidden, or interrupted
- what object or sign carries the tension

Environment:
- where the action occurs
- visual evidence of recent events
- lighting and atmosphere tied to the emotional beat

Tone:
- wonder, dread, intimacy, suspicion, grief, revelation

## Negative Prompt Focus

- action choreography that contradicts the scene
- smiling or triumphant mood during tense or uncertain beats
- random crowds not supported by canon
- duplicated characters, unreadable hands, broken eye lines
