# Character Image Template

## Best For

- protagonist portrait
- NPC portrait
- full-body character key art

## Prompt Structure

Subject:
- name and role in the story
- species or metaphysical nature when canonically known
- age impression or life stage when canonically known

Appearance:
- build, silhouette, posture
- face, hair, skin, scars, celestial or ritual marks
- clothing, armor, relics, tools, insignia

Story Signals:
- allegiance
- current tension
- emotional state
- evidence of travel, faith, rank, or danger

Scene Support:
- immediate background only if it clarifies the character
- lighting that reinforces tone

## Negative Prompt Focus

- non-canon weapons or props
- modern technology
- generic fantasy armor when the setting is more ritual than military
- extra limbs, mangled hands, blurred face, duplicated accessories
