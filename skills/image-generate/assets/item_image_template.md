# Item Image Template

## Best For

- relics
- ritual tools
- symbolic props
- treasure or evidence illustrations

## Prompt Structure

Item Identity:
- name
- purpose
- owner or faction association

Physical Read:
- silhouette
- material
- wear, age, sacred marks, inscriptions

Context:
- isolated plate or in-hand presentation
- background simplicity level

Story Signal:
- what the item says about belief, rank, memory, danger, or travel

## Negative Prompt Focus

- modern manufacturing details
- irrelevant ornament overload
- unreadable engravings presented as important
- floating geometry, broken symmetry, duplicated parts
